Student Loan Complaints Against Navient Overwheming Majority Following CFPB Lawsuit

by David Smith, Dec. 2023

In 2017, Navient Corporation became embattled in a series of lawsuits pertaining to their mishandling of student loans. Through multiple settlements, over 400,000 borrowers were deemed eligible for financial restitution as a result of Navient's mismanagement. This number encompasses over 66,000 private student loan borrowers, and approximately 350,000 federal loan borrowers.


Within these lawsuits, Navient was alleged to have incorrectly applied student loan payments to their corresponding accounts, directed borrowers towards predatory repayment plans and decieved borrowers about cosigner release requirements.


In the graph above, it shows the number of complaints against Navient since 2017, when the first five state attorney generals filed lawsuits against Navient. The overwhelming majority of complaints are related to student loan issues, signifying the disproportionate amount of mismanagement in regards to student loans.
