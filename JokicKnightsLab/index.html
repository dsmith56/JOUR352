A Look Into the Career of Nikola Jokic



Nikola Jokic has experienced one of the most unlikely rises to stardom. Born in Serbia, Jokic spent his first 20 years overseas before finally making it to the NBA. Throughout his career, Jokic has been labeled as "out of shape," "poor defender," "soft," and more as a result of his unorthodox build. Jokic's height and mental prowess have allowed him to overcome his athletic limitations and become recognized as one of the most iconic centers of this generation.
